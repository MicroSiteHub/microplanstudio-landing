About MicroPlan Studio

Simple tools to help you plan, organize, and accomplish more.

Our Mission

To provide simple, free, and privacy-friendly planning tools that help anyone structure their day, goals, health, and home—without complexity, subscriptions, or tracking.

What We Do

MicroPlan Studio builds lightweight, focused micro-tools designed around a single principle: planning should be simple.

Our generator tools help you create beautiful, printable planners in seconds. Whether you're planning your daily schedule, tracking habits, budgeting, organizing your home, or pursuing fitness goals—each tool is designed to be straightforward and distraction-free.

Our Approach

We believe that effective planning doesn't require complexity. Each tool focuses on one clear task: let you customize your planner and download it as a clean PDF. No login. No app. No tracking. Just you, your plan, and the ability to print it and get to work.

Free, Simple, Privacy-Friendly

All MicroPlan Studio tools are completely free to use. We don't track personal data, require subscriptions, or sell user information. The tools run entirely in your browser, and your information never leaves your device.

Categories

Our growing collection of generators spans five main categories:

  • Productivity: Daily, weekly, and monthly planners, time blocking, project planning, and goal setting.
  • Home: Budget tracking, chore charts, cleaning schedules, and maintenance logs.
  • Learning: Study planners and reading logs to track your educational progress.
  • Wellness: Habit trackers, fitness planners, mood journals, sleep logs, water intake trackers, and gratitude journals.
  • Specialized: Travel planning, finance tracking, and mindfulness tools.
